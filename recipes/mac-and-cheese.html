Mac and Cheese Recipe

Description

Enjoy this delicious homemade mac and cheese recipe!

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter and flour
  • Cheese
  • Milk
  • Seasonings
  • Bread Crumbs

Steps to make mac and cheese

  1. Boil the noodles, drain, and transfer to a prepared baking dish
  2. Make the cheese sauce, pour sauce over noodles, and stir
  3. Make the topping, spread it over macaroni and cheese, and sprinkle with paprika
  4. Bake the mac and cheese until top is golden brown
