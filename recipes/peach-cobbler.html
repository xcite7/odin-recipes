Southern Peach Cobbler Recipe

Description

Enjoy this fast and simple peach cobbler recipe!

This recipe will get you a delicious dessert in no time at all

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Peaches

Steps to make peach cobbler

  1. Make flour mixture
  2. Melt butter
  3. Add peaches and toss
  4. Bake 30 to 45 min at 350 degrees F
